Questions for interviews: 
- Semistructured
- Remote video interview, online interview (OBS or Screentomatic)

Consent form
Hello, my name is... First of all, do I have your consent to record this conversation for university purposes? Your response will be used for our research and your participation is voluntary, so if you want to withdraw from this at any time, you are allowed to. Also, you have the right to request from us not to upload your responses. Is that all right with you?


QUESTIONS
***Getting to know you questions****
1. Can you please introduce yourself?
2. Do you have a job or work occupation?
3. What does your typical day look like?
4. How do you travel to university?
5. How often do you cycle to school?
6. How much time does it take you to travel from home to work/school?
7. What are the biggest challenges that you face while traveling to university?

***General questions***
1. Do you think that weather affects your journey to university? How?
2. Have you ever been caught in the rain while going to university?
3. Do you use any apps to check the weather before going to university? Which ones?
4. How often do you use your smartphone to check the weather?
5. If you use any weather apps, what features do you find the most valuable?
6. Do you have any tips that help you stay dry while going out in rainy weather?
7. How likely are you to use an app that tracks rain and helps you plan your journey to university accordingly?

Thank you so much. This is the end of our interview.
